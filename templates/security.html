Security and trust principles

DevelobMail is built for practical email workflow education and tool discovery. We keep the public site simple, avoid asking for unnecessary sensitive data, and clearly separate editorial content from affiliate relationships.

Human review matters

We recommend AI-assisted workflows that keep a human approval step before important customer, subscriber, or partner emails are sent.

Analytics and comments

We use analytics to understand page performance and Disqus for comments. These third-party services may process data under their own policies.

Affiliate transparency

When partner links are used, we disclose them. Recommendations should be evaluated against your own privacy, budget, and compliance needs.
